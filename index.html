The Venue Advantage

Designed to accommodate the grandest of visions without compromising on comfort or convenience.

Grand Function Hall

A massive pillarless or elegantly structured main hall with seating capacity for over 1,000 guests. Perfect for grand nuptials and stage events.

Ample Parking Space

Never worry about guest convenience. Our undeniably large, hassle-free parking area ensures a smooth arrival and departure for hundreds of vehicles.

Separate Dining Hall

A dedicated dining space that comfortably serves 400-500 guests simultaneously, ensuring smooth catering operations away from the main event.

Flexible & Accommodating

Open 24 hours to handle traditional multi-day event setups. Renowned for accommodating management and flexible, customer-friendly pricing.
